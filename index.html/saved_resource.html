Our story

A Cafe Born from Passion

The Golden Cup opened its doors in 2015 with a single belief: that a truly exceptional cup of coffee, made with care and served with warmth, can make someone's entire day better.

Our founder Elena spent years traveling through Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — learning from the farmers who grow the world's finest beans. She came back with relationships, recipes, and a vision.

Every pastry is baked before sunrise. Every vegetable comes from farms within 100 miles. Every dish is tasted before it reaches your table. That's not a policy — it's who we are.
